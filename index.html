My last travelling, or how to try not to ruin everything and to save a good mood.

This year we came to Dragobrat again... Until recently, I didn`t believe that everything worked out. So we bought tickets at the station. That was a real adventure. We came to station and started find 6 tickets.Only 6.Why it`s a problem? Buut. Here we are! I was too nervous, because of my friends. One girl can`t go on that dates, another one on diffent ones. What to do? I awnted to let things go... Let fate decide! And she was cruel! We decided the dates and they were disgusting. But we didn`t have another variant. And 5 minutes before our leaving, I went to the site and checked for the last time for a new carriage! Oh,fortune!

Here I am at home looking through what would be all that are needed, checking the names and times. But I still couldn`t realize that we are going. And in general, there were many nuances in order to spoil our rest: almost everyone caught a cold,didn`t take much pills, the state of health was constantly not great; the weather wasn`t flying, mountain Stog didn`t work, foggy, strong wind. However we overcame all the difficulties, we managed to relax. And as a resulgt, there is something to remember. A month has already passed, and I still live in the meantime and those emotions. I fall asleep thinking about the mountains, and wake up thinking about a cool movement and a real atmosphere.
